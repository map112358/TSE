Mission Statement
The purpose of the TSE shall be to help foster a startup mentality among software-centric majors at the University of Missouri. The TSE shall:
  • Promote collaboration among students
  • Provide counsel and direction to new student-led startups
  • Connect student-led startups with potential outside investors/investment
